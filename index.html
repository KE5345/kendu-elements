Kendu Elements celebrates the project’s reimagining after the community takeover in early 2025. Just 410 items will be available, with the majority of proceeds going directly to Kendu’s official treasury. Designed from the outset to be a community-run NFT project focused on long-term sustainability, below are some of the key aspects.

Features

Meaningful art

The "blueprint" theme is a reflection of Kendu still being at a nascent stage and very much an open canvas. Recognisable elements from the ecosystem (Kendu Energy, Coffee, murals etc.) were then individually added to each NFT.

60% of every sale goes to the Kendu treasury

In order to support the wider Kendu ecosystem, 60% of revenue is allocated to the official Kendu treasury. This occurs automatically during the minting process using audited smart contracts.

Heavy discounts for existing Chad holders

For a limited time, those that already hold Chads can mint at a 75% lower price over other wallets.

Snapshot governance

As a community-run NFT project, holders can participate in Snapshot voting to coordinate and steer its direction. Non-binding voting for general Kendu-related topics will also be carried out as a means to gauge community sentiment in the overall ecosystem.

Focused on long-term growth

By building on a tried-and-true community foundation, where even the smallest holder can participate in meaningful decisions, the project will be in the best possible position for success over a long horizon.
